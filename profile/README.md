# Onsetto

**Business Account Switching and Relationship Activation for Financial Institutions**

Onsetto is a Business Switching and Relationship Activation Platform that helps financial institutions capture the **core operating account** and establish primacy by moving payroll, AR/AP, and operating cash flows into newly opened business accounts at onboarding.

Opening a business account is easy.
Making it the primary operating account is not.

Onsetto solves that gap.

---

## What Onsetto Does

Financial institutions win new business accounts every day, but many never become the customer’s core operating account. When switching stalls, balances move slowly, treasury services are delayed, and relationships remain secondary.

Onsetto automates the switching of a business’s core operating account so banks can achieve faster funding, earlier primacy, and stronger long-term relationships.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Automated Switching

Onsetto guides and automates the migration of payroll, receivables, payables, and recurring operating flows into a newly opened business account. Businesses complete switching in days instead of months, while bankers gain full visibility into progress, blockers, and funding readiness.

### AI Switch Assistant

Guided, step-by-step support for businesses throughout the switching process. The AI Switch Assistant reduces friction, improves completion rates, and keeps momentum high during the critical onboarding window.

### Funding Recommendations

Onsetto provides actionable guidance on transferring funds to activate the new operating account with confidence, helping accounts fund faster and reach primacy sooner.

### Switch Progress Tracking

Real-time visibility into migration status for bankers and operations teams. No spreadsheets, no manual follow-ups, no guessing.

### Prospecting Intelligence

Statement-based insights that strengthen the top of the business banking funnel. By analyzing a recent business statement, Onsetto surfaces cash flow patterns, fee exposure, liquidity indicators, and treasury signals to help bankers qualify and prioritize the right opportunities.

### Treasury Insight

Identify treasury gaps, fee leakage, and cash management opportunities using real transaction behavior. Onsetto highlights where businesses can be better served, reinforcing engagement and long-term primacy.

## How It Works

1. A business opens a new account
2. Onsetto identifies components of the core operating account

4. Accounts fund faster
5. The bank establishes primacy early
